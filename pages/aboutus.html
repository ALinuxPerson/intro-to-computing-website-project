Our Story

We launched our franchise on February 14, 2022, after a food tasting that won us over. The early days were tough with low sales and high expenses, but we pushed through with patience, smart budgeting, and a lot of faith.

Three years later, we're still standing, still growing, and still grateful. One of our biggest joys is helping working students stay in school, and we're proud to support them.

Our team's dedication inspires us every day because they are the heart of our journey.
